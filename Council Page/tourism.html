The West Highland Way




In Bearsden the begining of the West Highland Way starts on it's 96 mile journey to Fort William. The scenery is beautifl and can be done in small managable sections or if you feel adventurous you could do the whole 96 miles. The fastest it has been completed is 14 hours, 14 minutes, 44 seconds.
